Our Story

We set out to master institutional finance with frontier AI for finance - the hardest problem we could find.

The intelligence we built for markets became something more. Systems designed to navigate financial uncertainty turned out to be capable of reasoning across any domain. What started as trading AI evolved into a proprietary AI architecture built on our validated superintelligence core.
