# Palm Inversiones

## What it is
An **Argentine fintech** that bundles three product surfaces into one mobile app:

1. **Investment app** — buy/hold ETFs, equities, and instruments through a broker partner.
2. **Portfolio tracking** — visualize your holdings, expense categories, and progress toward goals.
3. **Financial advice** — personalized guidance ("asesoramiento personalizado") gated as a paid tier.

## Positioning
- "Tu asesor financiero personal. Sin letra chica, sin sorpresas."
- The product narrative: *take an Argentine saver and convert them into an investor.* The page assumes the visitor already has the savings habit but no investing literacy.
- Two tiers visible on the landing:
  - **Gratuita** — "Gestión financiera inteligente" (the free tier).
  - **Pago** — "Asesoramiento personalizado de inversiones", USD **12.50 / mes**, "Acceso por invitación".

## Regulation & partners
- Regulated by **CNV** (Comisión Nacional de Valores — Argentine securities regulator).
- Broker partner: **Alfy Inversiones**.
- "Hecho en Argentina" — local trust signal, used in marquee + footer.

## Conversion goal of the landing
Push **App Store / Google Play download**. The narrative arc:
1. **Problem** — "Siempre tuvimos que mirar de lejos al mundo financiero. Hasta ahora."
2. **Process** — 4 letter-format steps (resumen bancario → cuentas claras → objetivos → portafolio).
3. **Pillars** — gratis vs pago.
4. **Calculator** — "Vos solo vs con Palm" interactive ROI.
5. **Security** — CNV + Alfy.
6. **CTA** — store buttons + `@palm.inversiones`.
