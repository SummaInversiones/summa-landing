# Voice

## One-line
**"El amigo que entiende de plata."** Editorial, friendly, disciplined. Castellano rioplatense with voseo.

## Rules
- **Voseo always.** "vos", "bajate", "anotate", "pasate", "movés", "elegís", "ahorrás", "llegás".
- **"Plata", not "dinero".** Argentine, warmer, less corporate.
- **Sin letra chica.** The site's stated promise — translate it into copy by being explicit about pricing ("USD 12.50 / mes"), regulation ("Regulado por CNV"), and assumptions ("Cálculo orientativo. Asume 0% sobre el ahorro y 15% anual con Palm.").
- **No exclamation marks** in headlines. Confidence sounds like a period, not an exclamation.
- **No emoji in body copy.** The Argentina flag (🇦🇷) is acceptable as a regional anchor in marquee/footer; nowhere else. Phone emoji (📱) was removed from CTA.
- **Keyword italicization.** One word per section gets `.kw` styling (IBM Plex italic medium gold). Used as a visual rhythm device, not for emphasis. Examples: "inversor", "mirar de lejos", "Hasta ahora", "cuentas claras", "objetivos", "portafolio", "personalizado", "cálculo", "prioridad", "hoy".

## Anti-patterns
- "Bienvenido a Palm" — removed as a redundant welcome block under the hero headline.
- "Acceso anticipado · Argentina 🇦🇷" badge pill — removed (version-label tell + emoji-country tell).
- "con IA" / "AI-powered" framing — removed deliberately during the rebrand.
- Centered hero headline + "Get started for free" — Summa-era SaaS template feel; Palm's hero is asymmetric and pushes to store.

## Tone calibration
- **Problem section** is allowed to be heavier, almost elegiac: "Siempre tuvimos que mirar de lejos al mundo financiero. Hasta ahora."
- **Process section** is matter-of-fact, instructional: "Tu resumen bancario. El punto de partida."
- **Pillars** are confident and parallel: "Gestión financiera inteligente." / "Asesoramiento personalizado de inversiones."
- **CTA-final** is direct, no hedging: "Tu futuro comienza hoy. El primer paso es saber dónde estás parado."
